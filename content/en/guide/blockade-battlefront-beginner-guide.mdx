<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Controls and Skill Keys">

The biggest hurdle for new players is the sheer number of keybinds. This isn't just a "click and place" game; it's a "fight and survive" game. Your character's effectiveness is tied to your ability to execute moves at the right time.

**Standard Combat Keybinds:**
- **Primary Skills**: Q, E, R, T, Y
- **Utility & Defense**: F, G, H
- **Advanced Maneuvers**: Z, X, C, V, B
- **Mobility**: Double Spacebar (for flying or double jumping depending on the unit)

| Key | Typical Action | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Q / E** | Basic attacks or low-cooldown projectiles | High |
| **R / T** | Heavy attacks or area-of-effect (AoE) bursts | Medium |
| **F / G** | Defensive blocks, heals, or support summons | Essential |
| **Z / X** | Ultimate moves or high-mastery transformations | Strategic |
| **Space x2** | Dash, Leap, or Flight mechanics | Survival |

<Callout type="tip" title="Mastery Stacks">
Some units have "aerial variants" of their skills. Try using your Q or E skills while in mid-air (after a double spacebar) to see if the attack pattern changes. This is vital for dodging ground-based boss attacks.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Progression: Cens, Points, and Mastery">

Advancement in Blockade Battlefront is measured by your resources. You need to farm efficiently to unlock the higher-tier Titans and Special Units.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Redeem Active Codes">
    Enter the game and use the **!redeem** command. Current 2026 codes like **!redeem Return** (2,500 Points) and **!redeem Verified** (1,500 Points) provide a massive initial boost.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Mastery Cards">
    Mastery Cards grant instant levels to your units. **Always equip the unit you want to level up** before consuming a card, as the XP is tied to the active character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Farm Wave Clears">
    Standard waves are the best source of Cens. Focus on consistent clears rather than pushing "Insane" difficulty too early. A successful "Hard" run is better than a failed "Nightmare" run.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Resource | Primary Use | Best Source |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cens** | Shop purchases and unit upgrades | Mission farming and wave rewards |
| **Points** | Code rewards and early progression | !redeem commands and events |
| **Mastery** | Unlocking new skills and stat boosts | Using units in combat / Mastery Cards |
| **Zombie Core** | X-18 progression and Act 2 content | Zombie Mode V2 clears |
