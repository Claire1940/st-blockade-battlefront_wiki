<Section icon={Map} title="Core Gameplay Mechanics and Controls">

Blockade Battlefront is a unique hybrid experience on Roblox that merges wave-based tower defense with intense survival shooter action. Unlike traditional tower defense games where you simply watch units fight, here you are an active participant on the battlefield. You must deploy units and Titans while simultaneously using your own character's abilities to repel escalating waves of enemies.

Success in this **blockade battlefront guide** starts with mastering the complex control scheme. Your effectiveness in high-pressure modes like Boss Rush or Zombie V2 depends entirely on your ability to cycle through skills while managing unit placement.

| Category | Keybinds | Function |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Skills** | Q, E, R, T, Y | Core offensive and defensive abilities |
| **Secondary Skills** | F, G, H, Z, X | Utility, buffs, and secondary attacks |
| **Advanced Skills** | C, V, B | High-mastery or character-specific moves |
| **Movement** | Double Spacebar | Enhanced jumps or flight (character-dependent) |
| **Management** | /e vote | Initiate server restart or critical votes |

<Callout type="tip" title="Pro Tip: Skill Stacking">
Many high-tier characters have hidden aerial variants or skill stacks. Always test your abilities while jumping or during a dash to see if the animation or damage properties change.
</Callout>

### The Gameplay Loop
The core loop involves three distinct phases:
1.  **Preparation**: Spending Cens or Points to purchase new units or upgrade existing mastery.
2.  **Deployment**: Placing units strategically around the base to create a kill zone.
3.  **Active Defense**: Using your character's move set to eliminate stragglers and focus-fire on mini-bosses.

Mastery is character-specific. Using a unit consistently in battle is the most reliable way to level it up. At Mastery level 25, several units (like the Scientist CCTV Man) unlock powerful new forms or mechs.

<Callout type="tip" title="Grinding Hack">
