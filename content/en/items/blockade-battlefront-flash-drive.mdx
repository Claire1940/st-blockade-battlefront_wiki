<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Obtain All UTCM Flash Drives">

To obtain the formidable Upgraded Titan Cameraman (UTCM), Upgraded Camera Strider, or Scientist Cameraman, players must collect standard flash drives numbered #1 through #6, along with a specialized Keycard. Each of these items drops from specific boss enemies encountered during normal waves or Boss Rush runs. 

Historically, these bosses had a 12.5% drop rate, but recent updates calibrated the drop rates to a steady 10% for most bosses. This means you will need to defeat these targets repeatedly to secure your drops.

| Item | Dropping Boss | Drop Rate | Hourly Shop Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Flash Drive #1** | Astro Toilet | 10% | 5,000 Cens |
| **Flash Drive #2** | G-Toilet V1 | 10% | 5,000 Cens |
| **Flash Drive #3** | G-Toilet V2 | 10% | 5,000 Cens |
| **Flash Drive #4** | G-Toilet V2.5 / Big Multiverse Toilet | 10% | 5,000 Cens |
| **Flash Drive #5** | Acid Skull Toxic Toilet | 10% | 5,000 Cens |
| **Flash Drive #6** | G-Decoy (Fake G-Toilet) | 12.5% | 5,000 Cens |
| **Keycard** | G-Toilet V3 | 10% | 5,000 Cens |

<Callout type="warning" title="The Flash Drive #6 Glitch Warning">
Many community members have reported a known bug where **Flash Drive #6** fails to drop during Boss Rush mode when defeating the G-Decoy. If you are struggling to secure this drop, it is highly recommended to grind normal waves (specifically waves 80 to 90) with a group, rather than relying solely on Boss Rush.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Unlocking UTTV with Special Flash Drives">

Unlocking the Upgraded Titan TV Man (UTTV) is considered one of the most demanding endgame achievements in *ST: Blockade Battlefront*. This quest requires a completely different set of collectibles: Flash Drives #A through #E, plus the rare Drive #SdFE0. 

These special drives are significantly more expensive to buy in the Hourly Shop and have unique, challenging drop conditions.

| Item | Dropping Boss / Condition | Drop Rate | Hourly Shop Cost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Drive #SdFE0** | TV-race teammate death (non-titan) | 5% | 15,000 Cens |
| **Drive #A** | Boss Rush / Specific Bosses | Low RNG | 35,000 Cens |
| **Drive #B** | Boss Rush / Specific Bosses | Low RNG | 35,000 Cens |
| **Drive #C** | Boss Rush / Specific Bosses | Low RNG | 35,000 Cens |
| **Drive #D** | Boss Rush / Specific Bosses | Low RNG | 35,000 Cens |
| **Drive #E** | Astro Destructor / Trapper | 10% | 75,000 Cens |

<Callout type="tip" title="Drive #SdFE0 Co-op Strategy">
Because **Drive #SdFE0** only drops when a non-titan TV-race teammate dies, you cannot collect your own dropped drive if you die. The best way to farm this is to enter a game with a trusted friend, both play as TV-race characters (like ETV or Poly), and take turns dying so the other can retrieve the drop.
</Callout>

To complete the UTTV assembly, you must also complete the Scientist Cameraman mission and own all five special drives. Because the Hourly Shop prices are exceptionally high, relying entirely on Cens can drain your resources rapidly. Combining active boss grinding with shop purchases is the most balanced approach.

</Section>
