To farm Shards efficiently, you must manage your resources carefully from the very first wave. Follow this step-by-step progression route to optimize your setup before the difficult waves arrive.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Setup and Jetpack Purchase">
    Spawn into the map and begin clearing the initial waves. Prioritize purchasing a **Jetpack** as soon as possible. This tool is your primary defensive safety net for repositioning when cornered by large groups of toilets.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Save Resources for Special Titan Request">
    Do not spend your early-game cash on minor upgrades. Save your funds through the first helicopter phase. By the arrival of the **second helicopter**, you must have enough cash saved to immediately purchase the **Special Titan Request**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Clear Flying Units with Radiant Attacks">
    When flying enemy units appear, do not waste time with standard attacks. Use your **Radiant ability**, which completely one-shots flying units. Clearing the skies quickly keeps your ground movement uninhibited.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Capitalize on the Night Cycle Boost">
    The night cycle begins around the **6 to 8-minute mark**. During this phase, Weird Shard drop rates are **multiplied by 4x**. Focus entirely on clearing the high volume of small ground units that swarm the map during this time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Night Cycle Strategy">
During the night cycle, the boss **Jeffrey** may spawn. **Do not attempt to fight Jeffrey.** Instead, use your high mobility to kite him while focusing all of your firepower on the small ground toilets. The small toilets are fast, easy to kill, and yield the highest density of Weird Shards.
</Callout>
