Once the transmitter is activated, the Glitch Skibidi Toilet enters the battlefield. This boss behaves differently from standard enemies, relying on extreme speed and high-impact physical attacks to dismantle defenses. He does not use ranged projectiles but instead focuses entirely on physical disruption.

The boss continuously executes a high-speed "Ramming" attack. He targets the closest player or unit, rushes forward to strike, pauses briefly to recover, and then repeats the cycle. This relentless movement makes him difficult to target with slow-firing units.
