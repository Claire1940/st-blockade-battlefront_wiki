<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Solo Farming Strategy">

Farming the **blockade battlefront boss rush** solo requires a disciplined approach to resource management. You cannot afford to waste Cens on low-tier units that will be destroyed in the first three minutes. Follow this progression to maximize your efficiency and survival rate.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Pre-Game Preparation">
    Equip your best Mastery Cards and activate a 2x Mastery Boost if available. Ensure your keybinds are configured for comfort, as you will be using the Q, E, and R keys constantly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Early Phase Economy">
    Deploy 2-3 high-efficiency DPS units like the "Amplifier Woman" to handle the initial boss waves. Focus on saving Cens for your first Titan deployment rather than over-leveling basic units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Titan Deployment">
    As the fourth boss approaches, deploy your primary Titan (e.g., Upgraded Titan Speakerman). Position it centrally to allow for maximum coverage of the arena.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Skill Rotation Mastery">
    Cycle through your active abilities. Use your stun (F/G keys) to interrupt boss "charging" animations. Follow up with your heavy damage skills (Z/X keys) while the boss is incapacitated.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="The Final Push">
    During the final boss encounter, use all remaining Cens to deploy support Striders. Focus entirely on the boss's health bar and ignore minor adds unless they are directly threatening your base.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Mastery Grinding">
If your goal is purely Mastery farming, it is often more efficient to reset after the 5th boss. The difficulty spikes significantly at Boss 6, and the time-to-kill (TTK) increases, lowering your overall Mastery-per-minute ratio.
</Callout>

</Section>
