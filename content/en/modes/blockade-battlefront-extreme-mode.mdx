<Section icon={Target} title="Enemy Variants and Wave Analysis">

The enemies in **blockade battlefront extreme mode** are significantly more diverse than those found in lower tiers. Understanding the specific threats posed by different "Toilet" variants allows players to adjust their targeting priority (First, Last, Strongest, Weakest) effectively.

| Enemy Type | Primary Threat | Counter Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Glitch Toilet** | Extreme speed, bypasses slow zones | High-frequency stun units |
| **Giant Scientist** | Massive HP pool, spawns smaller units | Splash damage / AoE Titans |
| **Vacuum Toilet** | Can disable nearby units for 3 seconds | Long-range snipers / Support immunity |
| **Astro Toilet** | Flying unit, ignores ground-only towers | Anti-air specialized units |

**Wave Progression Breakdown:**
- **Waves 1-10**: Early economy phase. Use cheap, efficient defenders.
- **Waves 11-25**: Mid-game transition. Start placing your primary DPS units.
- **Waves 26-40**: Support phase. Maximize buffs and add slowing towers.
- **Waves 41-50**: The "Endgame." Every placement must contribute to maximum DPS.

<Callout type="info" title="Wave Strategy">

<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Placement Strategy">

A successful run in **blockade battlefront extreme mode** follows a strict timeline. Deviating from this placement order often results in an early exit during the mid-game boss waves.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Early Eco Setup">
    Place your first Economy unit immediately. Use a cheap defender (like the Basic Cameraman) at the very start of the path to handle the first 5 waves while you funnel all remaining cash into Economy upgrades.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Mid-Path Defense">
    By Wave 12, place your first mid-tier DPS unit at a strategic corner. This unit should be able to solo up to Wave 20 while you finish maxing out your Economy units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Support Hub Creation">
    Around Wave 25, establish your central "Buff Hub." Place your DJ Speakerman and any slow/stun units here. This area will eventually house your Titans.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Titan Deployment">
    Between Waves 30 and 40, begin deploying your Mythic or Godly units. Focus on upgrading one to max level before placing the next to benefit from the massive stat jumps at final upgrade levels.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Ability Management">
    During the final 5 waves, keep your hand on the active ability hotkeys. Use time-slows or global nukes only when the Boss Toilet reaches the halfway point of the map.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Victory Tip">
If playing in a team, designate one player as the "Eco/Support" lead and the others as "Heavy DPS." This specialization prevents redundant unit placements and maximizes map space.
</Callout>

</Section>
