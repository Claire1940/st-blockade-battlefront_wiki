<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Survival Strategies">

To conquer **blockade battlefront hell mode**, you must master the art of "Leaking Management." It is often better to let one small enemy pass if it means saving your high-damage abilities for the massive boss following behind it. 

### Early Game Economy
In Hell Mode, your starting cash is limited. Do not spend it all on one expensive unit. Instead, spread your defense across the path to maximize the time enemies spend under fire. 

| Strategy Phase | Action Item | Goal |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Waves 1-10** | Economy Building | Place low-cost units and upgrade them once |
| **Waves 11-20** | Mid-Game Pivot | Sell starters for mid-tier DPS; add CC |
| **Waves 21-30** | End-Game Setup | Max out Mythic units; prioritize Boss DPS |
| **Wave 30+** | Survival Mode | Use active abilities on cooldown; focus Boss |

<Callout type="tip" title="Active Ability Timing">
Do not use your ultimate abilities as soon as they are off cooldown. Wait for the 'Enraged' state of the boss or when a swarm of 'Fast' type enemies appears.
</Callout>

### Map Positioning
Positioning units at "U-turns" or "Loops" in the map is essential. This allows your units to hit the same enemy multiple times as they travel around the bend. In Hell Mode, every second of extra fire-time counts toward a successful clear.
