<Section icon={Target} title="Getting Started: Step-by-Step Match Setup">

Entering your first match can be overwhelming due to the variety of units and controls available. Following a structured approach to the early game will ensure you have the economy and defensive line needed for the later, more chaotic waves.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Join the Experience and Lobby">
    Launch the game via Roblox and enter a live server. Use the lobby area to check your current loadout and ensure your Mastery points are assigned to your primary units.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Normal Difficulty">
    Navigate to the mode selection interface. For beginners or those farming base resources, selecting the standard wave defense mode is recommended to learn enemy pathing.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initial Unit Placement">
    At the start of Wave 1, place your most cost-effective units near the enemy spawn points or at choke points leading to your base. Focus on units with high fire rates to handle initial scouts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Active Move Rotations">
    Use your keyboard (Q, E, R, T, Y, F, G, H, Z, X, C, V, B) to cycle through offensive abilities. Practice the double spacebar for vertical mobility to avoid ground-based enemy attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Titan Integration">
    As you reach Wave 10 and beyond, enemies will become tankier. This is the optimal time to deploy a Titan, such as the Titan Cameraman, to provide heavy fire support and area-of-effect damage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Avoid spending all your Cens on a single expensive unit early in the game. A swarm of weak enemies can easily bypass a lone powerful unit if it lacks the attack speed to clear the crowd.
</Callout>
