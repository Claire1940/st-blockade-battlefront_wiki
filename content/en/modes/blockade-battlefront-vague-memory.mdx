<Section icon={Swords} title="Phase Progression and Boss Mechanics">

Survival in this mission is divided into two distinct environmental phases [1]. Managing your resources and preparation during the transition is critical to surviving the escalation [1].

### Phase 1: Day Phase
The mission begins in the daytime. You start with 3,500 MiS to purchase initial upgrades and establish your defense [1]. Waves are replaced by an upward-counting survival timer [1]. During this phase, you must prepare for four possible boss spawns [1].

### Phase 2: Night Phase
After approximately 5 to 8 minutes of survival, your screen will fade to black [1]. When vision returns, the arena transitions to night [1]. The difficulty spikes dramatically as enemy health and damage multipliers rise to 15x [1]. To compensate, Weird Shard drops are multiplied by four [1].
