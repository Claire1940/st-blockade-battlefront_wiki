Mastery is the core progression system in Blockade Battlefront. For the Dark Amplifier, Mastery levels unlock the true potential of its hybrid kit, increasing the range of soundwaves and the lethality of its melee strikes.

**Mastery Progression Rewards:**
- **Level 1-10**: Basic damage increases and reduced cooldowns for Sound Blast.

- **Level 21-30**: Unlocks advanced Mastery Cards that can be equipped for specialized buffs.
- **Level 31+**: Significant scaling for late-game modes like Astro Invasion and Nightmare difficulty.
