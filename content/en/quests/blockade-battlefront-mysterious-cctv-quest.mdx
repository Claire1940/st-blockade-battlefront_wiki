<Section icon={Target} title="Core Requirements for the Mysterious CCTV Quest">

Once you successfully interact with the quest giver, you will be presented with four daunting tasks. These tasks are designed to test your progression, combat skill, and resource management. You cannot simply buy the Head Captain with Robux; you must prove your worth through gameplay.

| Task Name | Requirement | Objective |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ashes of the Inferno** | 200 Scorching Embers | Collect rare embers from the shop or Hellwaves |
| **Hell Conqueror** | Survive Hellwave | Must use a **non-titan CCTV character** |
| **Points Overload** | 80,000 Points | Accumulate total points through general gameplay |
| **Ally in Shadows** | Espada #1 Mastery | Reach **Level 80+ Mastery** with Espada #1 |

<Callout type="tip" title="Character Restriction">
For the **Hell Conqueror** task, the "Villain Arc Brown Cameraman" does **not** count toward the requirement. Stick to standard CCTV units to ensure the quest progress tracks correctly.
</Callout>

To complete these tasks efficiently, you should focus on multi-tasking. Farming points often coincides with surviving waves, and gathering Scorching Embers is a byproduct of high-level wave defense.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Farming Scorching Embers and Espada Mastery">

The two most time-consuming parts of the **blockade battlefront mysterious cctv quest** are gathering the 200 Scorching Embers and leveling the Espada #1. Scorching Embers are a rare currency that doesn't drop from every enemy, requiring a focused farming strategy.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Check the Global Shop">
    Scorching Embers occasionally appear in the shop for **5,000 credits**. This is the fastest way to bridge the gap if you have excess currency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Grind the Hellwave">
    Completing Hellwaves is the primary active method for obtaining embers. Ensure you are geared for high-intensity combat, as these waves are significantly harder than standard ones.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Espada #1 Leveling">
    Equip Espada #1 and focus on kills. Mastery is gained through active use. Reach Level 80 to satisfy the "Ally in Shadows" requirement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Point Accumulation">
    While farming Embers and Mastery, you will naturally gain the 80,000 points needed. Do not spend them until the quest acknowledges the total.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Espada Mastery Tip">
Reaching Level 80 Mastery is a significant grind. It is recommended to farm in areas with high enemy density to maximize experience gain per minute.
</Callout>

</Section>
