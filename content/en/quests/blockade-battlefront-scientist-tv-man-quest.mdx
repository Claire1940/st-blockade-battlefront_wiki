### How to Obtain the Dark Drive
The Dark Drive is a specialized quest item that cannot be bought directly from the standard shop. It has a **10% drop chance** whenever a TV-race team member (excluding Espada and Titan TV Man) dies in battle. 

| Item Name | Drop Source | Drop Rate | Estimated Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dark Drive (#SdFE0)** | TV-race characters (Normal TV, Scientist TV, Large TV) | **10% (1/10)** | 15,000 Cens (Hourly Shop) |
| **Flashdrive #A - #D** | Boss Rush / Hard Mode Bosses | **Varies** | 35,000 Cens each |
| **Flashdrive #E** | Astro Rocketeer, Trapper, Clone G | **10% (1/10)** | 75,000 Cens |

<Callout type="warning" title="Anti-Self Pickup Rule">
You cannot pick up your own dropped Dark Drive. You must queue with a trusted friend, play as TV-race characters, and pick up each other's drops when defeated.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Step-by-Step Quest Walkthrough">

Once you hand over the Dark Drive to Scientist TV Man, he will deem you worthy and unlock the official questline. This massive progression track requires completing a series of combat trials, badge acquisitions, and tactical challenges.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate the Space Toilet Mission">
    Talk to Scientist TV Man to be teleported to the custom Toilet Space map. Your goal is to navigate the corridors, avoid triggering heavy enemy reinforcements, and rescue the captured alliance scientist.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Defeat the Space Mech Boss">
    At the end of the Space Toilet map, a massive boss will spawn. Target its eyes, jetpack, and laser mounts to disable its heavy offensive skills. Defeating the boss without calling reinforcements awards a bonus **2,000 Points**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Track the Infected Titan Speaker Man">
    Accept the next phase from Scientist TV Man. Purchase a tracker for **500 Points** and attach it to the back of the Upgraded Infected Titan Speakerman during a live match to unlock the "Location Found" badge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defend the TV Base Invasion">
    Enter the newly unlocked casual mode to defend the TV Base from a malware toilet invasion. Call in Camera or TV Men reinforcements to secure the perimeter and survive the final waves.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Solo Progression Strategy">
For the Space Mech Boss fight, weave in and out of cover. Do not stand in the open, as its laser sweep can deplete your health pool in under three seconds.
</Callout>

</Section>

### Efficient Farming Routes
To optimize your grinding speed, avoid standard defense mode when hunting for drives. Use the targeted methods outlined below:

| Target Item | Ideal Game Mode | Recommended Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Flashdrives A-D** | Boss Rush | Run with a coordinated team; do not transform into a Titan so you can easily spot and pick up physical drops on the floor. |
| **Keycards** | Hard Mode | Target G-Man 3.0 specifically, ignore smaller toilets, and burst him down quickly. |
| **Drive E** | In The Mist / Astro Waves | Farm the specific Astro and Trapper waves up to Wave 6, then reset for maximum efficiency. |
