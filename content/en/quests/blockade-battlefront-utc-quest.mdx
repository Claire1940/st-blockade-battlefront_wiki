<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step UTC Unlock Process">

Unlocking the Upgraded Titan Cameraman is not a single-task endeavor. It involves a series of progression gates that test your knowledge of the game's mechanics, from currency farming to high-difficulty wave survival. Follow these steps to complete the **blockade battlefront utc quest** efficiently.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Reach Mastery Level 25">
    Before the UTC quest becomes fully active, you must demonstrate proficiency with the Cameraman faction. Reach Mastery Level 25 with base units like the Scientist Cameraman or Plungerman. This unlocks the ability to pilot larger mech-style units and eventually the Titans.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Cens and Points">
    Endgame quests are resource-intensive. You will need a significant amount of Cens (the primary progression currency) to purchase the necessary quest items from the shop. Use active codes to jumpstart your economy and focus on Boss Rush mode for high-yield Cens farming.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Complete Zombie V2 Act 2">
    The UTC quest is closely tied to the Zombie Mode progression. You must clear Act 2 of the Zombie V2 update, which involves surviving infected waves and defeating the Act 2 Boss. This proves your character can handle the pressure required to command a Titan.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Collect Titan Core Materials">
    During your runs in Astro Invasion or Zombie Mode, enemies will drop rare core materials. You need to collect specific "Camera Cores" to stabilize the UTC's energy output. These are low-drop-rate items, so using a 2x Mastery or Luck boost is highly recommended.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Final Titan Request">
    Once all requirements are met, navigate to the Special-Titan-Request menu. Here, you will submit your materials and Mastery proof to finally unlock the Upgraded Titan Cameraman for use in all game modes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Do not spend your Cens on cosmetic skins until the UTC quest is complete. The resource requirements for the final step are steep, and wasting currency early can delay your unlock by several days of grinding.
</Callout>
