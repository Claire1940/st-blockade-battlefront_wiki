The **blockade battlefront utcm quest** is divided into four distinct phases, each testing different aspects of your unit roster, mechanical skill, and teamwork. While some steps require raw survival time, others demand specific character mastery or coordinated team play.

Below is the structured, step-by-step breakdown of each part of the quest, followed by an optimized overview of recommended strategies for each stage.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Part 1: CCTV Roster & Endless Wave 70">
    Your first goal is to build a solid foundation. You must own almost every standard Cameraman unit in the game, excluding late-game variants like Glitchmobile or Chief Scientist. Additionally, you must equip the basic Titan Cameraman (TCM) and survive up to Wave 70 in Endless Mode, while activating the Titan Request feature 10 times across your runs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Part 2: Plungerman Survival & Target Shoots">
    This phase shifts focus to Boosted Plungerman (also referred to as Jetpack Plunger). You need to survive Wave 70 on Endless and Wave 15 on Hard difficulty as Boosted Plungerman. Furthermore, you must survive for 600 consecutive seconds (10 minutes) in a live match without getting downed once, and use Camerawoman's Target-Shoots ability 30 times.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Part 3: The Multi-Titan Alliance">
    This is often considered the most chaotic phase. You must join or coordinate a lobby where at least six Titan Cameramen are active on the battlefield simultaneously. While equipped with Titan Cameraman, use the Titan Request 5 times, and use Boosted Plungerman's "Declaration" (motivate buff) on a Titan Cameraman or UTCM player 10 times.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Part 4: Boss Hunting & Final Purchase">
    The final phase requires you to defeat 10 G-Toilet 2.0 (Gman V2) bosses and 10 Infected Titan Speaker bosses. These kills accumulate across matches, meaning you do not have to defeat them all in a single run. Once completed, return to the Engineer Cameraman to buy the UTCM for 3,000 Cens or Points.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="How to Farm Flashdrives and Keycards">

In older versions of ST: Blockade Battlefront, players had to collect six physical Cog Shards to unlock the Upgraded Titan Cameraman. However, updates have replaced cogs entirely with **Flashdrives #1-6** and a specialized **Keycard**. These items are stored in your inventory (accessible by pressing 'M' on PC) and are mandatory for the final assembly of the UTCM.

Each Flashdrive and Keycard has a specific 10% to 12% drop rate from designated bosses. Alternatively, if you find yourself struggling with bad random number generator (RNG) drops, you can purchase them from the **Hourly Shop** in the lobby for 5,000 Cens each.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="UTCM Abilities and Mastery System">

Once you complete the **blockade battlefront utcm quest** and purchase the Upgraded Titan Cameraman, you gain access to one of the most powerful frontline tanks in the game. UTCM has two primary combat states: **Normal Mode** and **Battle Mode**. Swapping modes changes your skill set and allows you to adapt to different enemy wave patterns.

His signature Gravitational Cannon pulls enemies close before smashing them, while his core-emitted Blue Flames melt grouped enemies with an intense burning damage-over-time (DOT) effect.

| Ability Name | Combat Mode | Damage Profile | Cooldown | Combat Utility |
| **Gravitational Cannon** | Battle Mode | 500 Damage | 6 Seconds | Attracts enemies and slams them down |
| **Blue Flames** | Battle Mode | 51 Damage per tick + DOT | Continuous | Melting grouped enemies and small waves |
| **Clap Strike** | Battle Mode | 2,500 Damage | 10 Seconds | High-impact burst damage to bosses |
| **Jetpack Crash** | Battle Mode | 3,500 Damage | 15 Seconds | Aerial dive, heavy AoE crowd control |

To maximize UTCM's potential, you must invest in his **Mastery System**. As you gain Mastery levels by using him in battle or applying Mastery Cards, you unlock powerful perks:
- **Mastery 25**: Unlocks the upgraded shield, granting complete immunity to projectile missiles and reducing incoming damage.
- **Mastery 50**: Increases basic punch combo damage and extends the range of the Gravitational Cannon.
- **Mastery 80**: Unlocks the overcharged core state, boosting the damage of Blue Flames and reducing all active cooldowns.
