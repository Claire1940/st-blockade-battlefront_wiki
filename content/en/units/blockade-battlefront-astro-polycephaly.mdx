<Section icon={Map} title="Mastering the Dark Dimension Mode">

The Dark Dimension is the primary gatekeeper for **blockade battlefront astro polycephaly**. This game mode consists of four distinct rounds, each introducing unique mechanics that can instantly end a run if ignored. Success in this mode is mandatory to trigger the appearance of the Astro Polycephaly circle at the end.

| Round | Primary Mechanic | Survival Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Round 1** | Orb Collection & Escape | Collect orbs quickly/Avoid Jeffrey traps |
| **Round 2** | Chasing Spikes | Stay in constant motion/Never stop moving |
| **Round 3** | Sanity Eye & Red Zones | Stop moving when eye appears/Avoid red zones |
| **Round 4** | Survival Timer | Zero mistakes allowed/Enter green circle at end |

In the third round, the "Squid Game" style mechanic is particularly lethal. When the eye icon appears on your screen, any movement will cause your sanity to plummet, leading to a high-speed pursuit by Jeffrey. Additionally, an Acid Boss will spawn during this phase, requiring precise dodging while managing your movement restrictions.

<Callout type="warning" title="Danger Zone">
During Round 4, the margin for error is non-existent. One mistake typically results in immediate death. Wait for the timer to expire and look for the green Astro Polycephaly circle to secure your victory.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="Farming Astro Tokens and Parts">

Gathering the 5 required Astro Tokens is often cited by the community as the most difficult part of the quest. These tokens are not direct drops; instead, they must be crafted by exchanging parts collected from the Astro Toilets mode. You must fill a progress bar to 100% at a specific NPC to receive a single token.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Enter Astro Toilets Mode">
    Queue for the specific Astro Toilets game mode. This is the only place where the necessary parts drop from defeated enemies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect Dropped Parts">
    Defeat Astro Toilets and look for glowing parts on the ground. You must manually pick these up to add them to your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Exchange NPC">
    Find the specialized NPC in the hub world who handles Astro technology.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Fill the Progress Bar">
    Hand over your collected parts. Every time the bar reaches 100%, you are awarded one Astro Token. Repeat this 5 times.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The drop rates for these parts are randomized, meaning some matches may yield multiple pieces while others provide none. Consistency is key to completing this grind.

| Item Type | Drop Source | Estimated Rarity |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Astro Arm** | Astro Toilet Elite | Rare |
| **Astro Core** | Astro Toilet Boss | Very Rare |
| **Astro Plating** | Standard Astro Toilet | Common |
| **Astro Token** | NPC Exchange | Guaranteed at 100% Bar |

<Callout type="tip" title="Efficiency Boost">
Play in a group to clear Astro Toilets faster. While the drops are individual, faster clear times mean more opportunities for parts per hour.
</Callout>
