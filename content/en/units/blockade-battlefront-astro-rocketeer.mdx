<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics & Attack Patterns">

Defeating the boss requires a deep understanding of its two distinct combat phases: the Aerial Phase and the Grounded Phase. The boss dynamically transitions between these behaviors depending on player distance and incoming projectile threats.

During the Aerial Phase, the boss hovers at medium height and rains down rockets. Each rocket deals a substantial 150 damage upon impact. If players attempt to counter with slow, high-damage projectiles, the boss will perform a quick dash to the left or right, easily evading the incoming attacks. When players get too close, the boss transitions to its Grounded Phase, executing a massive shockwave attack that deals 400 damage and pushes players back, resetting the distance.
