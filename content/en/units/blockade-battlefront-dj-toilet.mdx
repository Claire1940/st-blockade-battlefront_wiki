To maximize your efficiency with the **DJ Skibidi Toilet 2.0** on the battlefield, follow this optimal rotation and setup guide.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Activate Buffs First">
    Before engaging any wave or boss, activate your **Music Playback** to secure your passive 10% damage boost and health regeneration. Follow up immediately with the **Amplifier Buff** to prep your offensive skills.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Unleash Speaker Mode Burst">
    Switch to **Speaker Mode** and deploy your Soundwave and Sound-Blast. Utilize the Projectile Shield if you are facing ranged enemies like the Camera Attack Helicopter or TV units to reflect their attacks back at them.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Maintain Laser Pressure">
    While your Speaker abilities are on cooldown, hold down your **4x Laser Cannons** to melt remaining targets. Be sure to release the trigger before the heat meter maxes out to keep the cooldown at a minimal 2 seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Escape and Reposition">
    If your HP drops below 30%, switch to **Defensive Mode**. Use the dual Jetpack Dashes to quickly retreat to a safe distance, reactivate your Music Playback to heal, and prepare for your next offensive run.
  </Step>
</Steps>
