<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Unlocking the Upgraded Speaker Carrier">

The **Upgraded Speaker Carrier** is one of the most powerful support assets you can bring into the **blockade battlefront gman 4.0** fight. When summoned, it plays custom background music, heals nearby allies, and drops powerful Blaster Cars onto the battlefield to attack enemies.

### Unlock Requirements

Unlocking this variant requires heavy investment into your Speaker Faction unit masteries.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Level Up Speaker Units">
    Train at least **three different Speaker Faction units** to Mastery Level 80 or higher (e.g., Dark Speakerman, Elite Speakerman, Speakerwoman).
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip the Base Mastery">
    Ensure the standard Speaker Carrier ability is unlocked on your account via your character progression screen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Form the Faction Trio">
    Equip three qualified Level 80+ Speaker units in your active loadout. Note that Titan units do not count toward this unlock requirement.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Summon In-Game">
    Press the **U key** on your keyboard during a live match to deploy the Upgraded Speaker Carrier and replace the default healing variant.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Carrier Healing Buff">
The Upgraded Speaker Carrier heals standard units for 5% of their maximum health, but provides an increased **10% health healing** to all Speaker Faction units on the map.
</Callout>
