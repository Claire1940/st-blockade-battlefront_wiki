<Section icon={Map} title="Introduction to Blockade Battlefront Jeffrey">

In the intense wave-defense and survival-shooter environment of Roblox's *ST: Blockade Battlefront*, **blockade battlefront jeffrey** stands out as one of the most terrifying entities. Unlike standard units or bosses that you can wear down with high-damage builds, Jeffrey is classified as an **unkillable stage modifier**. He acts as an active environmental hazard designed to punish players who lose situational awareness or fail to manage their resources.

Derived from the mysterious lore of the TV Men faction, Jeffrey's mind has been completely warped by spending too much time within the void of the gate's realm. In *Blockade Battlefront*, this insanity manifests as two distinct, highly lethal phases that force players to completely alter their playstyle depending on the active dimension or mission phase.

<Callout type="warning" title="Hazard Warning">

### Form Comparison

| Feature | Small Form | Colossal Form |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Primary Location** | Dark Dimension void | Vague Memory Mode (Phase 2) |
| **Size Scale** | Normal TV Man size | Monumental / Map-spanning |

<Section icon={Shield} title="How to Survive Colossal Jeffrey in Vague Memory">

Colossal Jeffrey spawns during Phase 2 (Night) of the **Vague Memory** endless mission, shortly after the second Helicopter Shop takes off. Surviving this phase requires precise execution of defensive maneuvers.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Monitor the Phase Transition">
    As Phase 2 begins, prepare your squad for massive environmental hazards. Ensure all primary defensive units are placed away from the edges of the map where Jeffrey's slams are most likely to hit.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Listen for the Audio Cues">
    Before any major attack, Jeffrey will emit loud, agonizing screeches. When you hear these sound effects, immediately stop focusing on standard wave enemies and prepare to react.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Avert Your Camera Screen">
    When Jeffrey's screen begins to brighten, **immediately turn your camera away**. Do not allow any part of Jeffrey to be visible on your game screen. Looking at him during this flash results in an instant wipe that bypasses lenses and shields.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Dodge the Tracking Orbs">
    When Jeffrey fires his 4 purple orbs, use high-mobility units or dash mechanics to dodge. Each orb deals 1,000 damage, which can easily shred your squishier support units if they stand still.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Section>
