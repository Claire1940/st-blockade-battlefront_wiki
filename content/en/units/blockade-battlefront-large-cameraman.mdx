Understanding the nuances of the **blockade battlefront large cameraman** skill set is essential for maximizing your impact on the battlefield. Each ability has a specific rhythm that, when mastered, allows for seamless transitions between offense and defense.

### Primary Attack: Punch Combo
The bread and butter of your arsenal is the 5-hit punch combo. Each of the first four hits deals 45 damage, alternating between left and right hooks. The fifth hit is a heavy slam that provides a small area-of-effect (AOE) impact.

### Special Abilities
Beyond the basic punches, the Large Cameraman utilizes high-impact cooldown skills. The Kick provides immediate knockback, while the Dash ensures you are never out of position. The most complex skill, Grab, requires precise timing but offers the highest reward in multi-enemy engagements.

| Ability Name | Damage Output | Cooldown | Tactical Utility |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Punch Combo | 225 Total | None | Sustained DPS / Crowd Pressure |
| Forward Kick | 80 / 160 | 10 Seconds | Burst Damage / Creating Space |
| Dash | N/A | 5 Seconds | Gap Closing / Skill Evasion |
| Grab & Hurl | 100 + AOE | 15 Seconds | Enemy Displacement / Explosive Finish |

<Callout type="warning" title="Cooldown Management">
Avoid using your Dash and Kick simultaneously. If you dash into a crowd without your Kick or Grab ready, you may find yourself surrounded with no way to push enemies back or escape.
</Callout>

### Advanced Grab Mechanics
The Grab ability is more than just a single-target strike. Once you grab an enemy, they become a projectile. Hurling them into a group of enemies not only deals 100 damage to the thrown target but also applies that damage to any enemies hit by the collision. This makes it the most efficient way to deal with tightly packed Skibidi formations.

</Section>

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Reach Mastery Level 50">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reach Mastery Level 80">

  </Step>
