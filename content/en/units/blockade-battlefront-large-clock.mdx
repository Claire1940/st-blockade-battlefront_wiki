<Section icon={Swords} title="Detailed Ability Breakdown and Combat Mechanics">

The effectiveness of the **blockade battlefront large clock** depends on the player's ability to chain skills during the 11-second window of absolute control. While his melee attacks provide filler damage, his secondary and special abilities define his tier placement.

**Melee Combo**
The Large Clockman utilizes a two-hit fist combo. Each swing deals 50 damage. While this is his lowest damage output source, it is essential for finishing off low-health stragglers without wasting ability cooldowns.

**Bomb-Plant (Core DPS)**
This is the unit's primary offensive tool. Under normal conditions, it has a 4-second cooldown and deals 300 damage. However, its true power is unlocked during the Time-Stop state, where the cooldown is entirely removed.

**Warp (Mobility)**
With an 11-second cooldown, Warp allows the Large Clockman to teleport a short distance forward. This is vital for repositioning before initiating a Time-Stop or for escaping high-damage area-of-effect (AOE) zones from enemy bosses.

**Time-Stop-Throw (Crowd Control)**
By throwing an orb of pure time energy, the Large Clockman deals 200 damage and freezes enemies in place. This is a 25-second cooldown skill that acts as a mini-cc between major Time-Stop rotations.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Optimal Time-Stop Execution Strategy">

The "Bomb-Spam" technique is the definitive way to play the **blockade battlefront large clock**. Executing this correctly can deal thousands of damage in a single 11-second window.

1.  **Preparation**: Ensure your Warp is off cooldown.
2.  **Engagement**: Warp into the center of the largest enemy cluster.
3.  **Activation**: Trigger **Time-Stop**.
4.  **The Spam**: Press the **Bomb-Plant** button as fast as possible. Since there is no cooldown, you should be able to plant 10-15 bombs in the 11-second window.
5.  **Detonation**: Once Time-Stop ends, all bombs explode simultaneously.
6.  **Withdrawal**: Use Time-Stop-Throw to freeze any survivors and Warp back to safety.
