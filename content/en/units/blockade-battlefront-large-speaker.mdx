<Section icon={Shield} title="Introduction to the Large Speakerman">

The **blockade battlefront large speaker** (commonly known as the Large Speakerman) is one of the foundational units introduced in the Roblox wave-defense game. Towering over standard infantry, this heavy hitter is designed to anchor defensive lines by emitting powerful acoustic shockwaves and absorbing significant punishment. 

For new players, acquiring this unit is highly recommended. It provides a reliable mix of raw physical damage, crowd control, and durability, making early-game progression much more manageable. While it excels at ground control, players must strategically upgrade its skill tree to handle complex wave compositions.

| Feature | Specification | Playstyle Role |

| **Cost Efficiency** | Extremely High (Recommended Starter) | Base Defense & Wave Clear |
| **Main Drawback** | Low base anti-air capability without Jetpack | Ground-focused brawler |

<Section icon={Swords} title="Abilities and Move List">

The combat loop of the **blockade battlefront large speaker** relies on a mixture of heavy punches, soundwave manipulation, and environmental throws. Understanding the damage profiles and cooldowns of each ability is essential to maximizing your battlefield efficiency.

### Base Combat Abilities

| Ability Name | Description | Damage Output | Cooldown |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Basic Punches** | Two slow, heavy-hitting standard punches. | 120 Total (60 per hit) | None (Default Attack) |
| **Fast Kick Combo** | A rapid succession of quick hits finished with a kick. | 80 Damage | 11 Seconds |
| **Ground Slam** | Slams both arms down, crushing nearby ground targets. | 50 Damage | 11 Seconds |
| **Soundwave** | Emits an acoustic blast that damages and knocks back enemies. | 80 Damage (160 at Mastery 25+) | 25 Seconds |
| **Toilet Throw** | Grabs a small enemy, throwing them to cause an explosion. | 100 AoE Damage (Insta-kills target) | 22 Seconds |
| **Dash** | A quick directional dash used for positioning or dodging. | Utility / Movement | 3 Seconds |

### Advanced Upgraded Moves

Once you unlock specific nodes in the skill tree and equip a jetpack, you gain access to high-impact maneuvers:

| Move Name | Requirement | Damage Output | Cooldown |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Jetpack Tackle** | Jetpack equipped; tackles forward, ticking damage. | 220 Total (40 per tick) | 17 Seconds |
| **Knife Stab** | Knife Skill Tree node purchased. | 120 Damage | 13 Seconds |
| **Scream** | Sound Improvement Skill Tree node purchased. | 480 Damage | 11 Seconds |
| **Fly Slam** | Ground Slam Skill Tree node + Jetpack. | 150 Damage | 13 Seconds |

<Callout type="warning" title="Anti-Air Limitations">
Standard soundwaves and punches cannot hit high-altitude flying enemies. You must unlock the Jetpack and the Fly Slam ability to engage aerial targets effectively.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Mastery Perks and Progression">

Progression doesn't stop at the skill tree. Playing and surviving waves with this unit rewards you with Mastery Experience. Reaching specific milestones unlocks powerful passive buffs and summoning capabilities that transform this unit into a top-tier support tank.

### Mastery Milestones

*   **Mastery Level 25**: The damage of your core "Soundwave" ability is doubled (increasing from 80 to 160 damage), greatly improving your crowd-control efficiency.
*   **Mastery Level 50**: Standard progression milestone (no direct ability changes currently active).
*   **Mastery Level 80**: Unlocks access to the **Speaker Carrier** summon, allowing you to deploy automated support assets to hold down lanes.
