<Section icon={Swords} title="Base Abilities and Moveset Breakdown">

The foundation of playing the Speakerwoman effectively lies in mastering her base moveset. Her standard attacks provide a reliable mix of close-range defense and ranged pressure. Her primary attack, LMB, deals reliable damage to all enemies within her striking hitbox. This attack scales significantly as you progress through her mastery levels, making it a staple of her combat rotation.

Her secondary skills, such as Sound-wave and Knife-Shoot, offer excellent burst potential. Sound-wave acts as a panic button, clearing out immediate threats surrounding the player, while Knife-Shoot allows for precise targeted damage from a safe distance. For mobility, Leap offers a forward flip that helps dodge incoming projectiles or reposition during chaotic skirmishes. Her most powerful base utility comes from Musician, which grants temporary immortality and enhances the damage of her other skills, allowing for aggressive counter-plays.

| Ability | Damage | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **LMB** | 15 / 30 | Basic melee strike hitting all targets in hitbox |
| **E - Sound-wave** | 200 | AOE sound wave dealing burst damage |
| **R - Knife-Shoot** | 300 total | Fires 2 knives dealing 150 damage each |
| **T - Leap** | 0 | Utility flip forward for mobility |
| **Y - Musician** | 0 (Buff) | Grants temporary immortality and damage buffs |
| **U - Speaker Carrier** | Varies | Summons healing carrier (Mastery 80+ required) |

<Section icon={Map} title="Katana Mode and Alternate Moveset">

When transitioning to Katana Mode, the Speakerwoman shifts her combat style toward high-speed melee engagements. This mode is designed for players who prefer a fast-paced, aggressive playstyle that prioritizes mobility and quick strikes over sustained ranged support.

In this mode, her basic LMB attack maintains its damage scaling but is delivered through swift sword strikes. The core of Katana Mode revolves around Domain-Breaker and Heavenly-Slash. Domain-Breaker unleashes a powerful AOE slash that punishes grouped enemies, making it ideal for clearing waves. Heavenly-Slash serves as both an offensive tool and a secondary mobility option, letting you dash forward while slashing through targets. Mastering the transition between her base kit and Katana Mode is essential for high-level play.

| Move | Damage Type | Tactical Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **LMB** | Melee | Quick strikes with standard damage scaling |
| **F - Domain-Breaker** | AOE | Clear surrounding enemies with burst damage |
| **G - Heavenly-Slash** | Dash | Close distances while dealing minor damage |
