<Section icon={Map} title="How to Unlock Upgraded Titan Cameraman">

Unlocking the Upgraded Titan Cameraman (UTC) is a rite of passage for any serious player. Unlike standard units, obtaining this special Titan requires completing a rigorous four-step questline given by the **Engineer Cameraman** in the lobby, collecting rare boss drops, and spending points.

<Callout type="warning" title="Quest Safety Alert">
Do not use the standard Titan Cameraman or Titan TV Man while attempting to die for specific quest requirements. Teammates using Plunger units can automatically heal you, which will prolong your attempts to trigger death-based quest flags.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate the Quest & Phase 1">
    Locate the Engineer Cameraman NPC standing in the corner of the lobby. Speak with him to accept the quest. Step 1 requires you to own all basic camera-type units, request the Titan Cameraman 10 times, and successfully survive to Wave 70 on Infinite Mode while playing as the Titan Cameraman.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master Jetpack Double Plunger">
    Once Step 1 is complete, proceed to Step 2. You must request the Titan Cameraman 5 times, survive as the Jetpack Double Plunger for 10 minutes without going down, beat Wave 70 on Infinite Mode, clear Wave 15 on Hard Mode, and successfully use Camerawoman's T-ability 30 times.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Server Synergy & Buffs">
    For Step 3, you must find a server containing at least six Titan Cameramen simultaneously. Additionally, request the Titan Cameraman 5 more times, and use the Jetpack Double Plunger's motivate buff on a friendly Titan Cameraman a total of 10 times.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Boss Hunting & Shard Collection">
    The final step requires you to defeat 10 Jman V2 bosses and 10 Infected Titan Speakermen. While completing these objectives, you must farm and collect 6 unique Shards/Cogs, which have a 10% drop rate from elite bosses. Once all items are collected, purchase the morph from the Titan menu for 3,000 Points.
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Titan Cameraman Ability & Damage Breakdown">

The Titan Cameraman relies on a mixture of heavy melee strikes, high-impact area-of-effect (AoE) slams, and defensive shielding to control the battlefield. Understanding the raw damage numbers and active keybinds is crucial for maximizing your performance during late-game zombie waves and boss encounters.

| Ability / Move | Damage Output | Keybind / Trigger | Primary Utility & Effects |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Punch Combo** | 175 DMG per hit (2 hits) | Left Click / Basic | Quick frontal sweep to clear low-tier enemies |
| **Foot Stomp** | 250 DMG (Base) | Active Keybind | Ground slam that deals AoE damage to small targets |
| **Jetpack Dash** | 600 DMG per hit (Chain x2) | Active Keybind | High-mobility gap closer and escape tool |
| **Leap & Slam** | 750 (Leap) / 1,500 (Slam) | Active Keybind | Heavy crowd-control attack with massive AoE radius |
| **Core Flame** | 30 DMG per tick + DOT | Active Keybind | Channeled blue fire stream that melts clustered waves |
| **Ballistic Shield** | Negates incoming damage | Active Keybind | Grants 80% damage resistance; disables Core Flame |
