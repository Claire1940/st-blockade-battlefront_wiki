<Section icon={Target} title="Unlocking the blockade battlefront tri titan">

Acquiring this unit requires dedication and a systematic approach to farming. Players must progress through a specific questline distributed by the Tri-Faction Coordinator located in the central hub. The process involves gathering rare materials from high-difficulty zones and defeating specific boss units.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate the Questline">
    Locate the Tri-Faction Coordinator in the Sector 4 Outpost. Accept the quest titled 'The Union of Three' to unlock the tracking of required materials.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Farm Faction Cores">
    Defeat the Cameraman Boss, Speakerman Boss, and TV Titan Boss in their respective raids to collect one Core from each faction.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gather Chrono Shards">
    Complete daily challenges and endless mode runs to accumulate the necessary amount of Chrono Shards required for assembly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Assemble the Titan">
    Return to the assembly station in the Sector 4 Outpost with all materials and pay the required gold fee to finalize the creation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
