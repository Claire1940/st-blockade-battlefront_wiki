<Section icon={Swords} title="Ability Breakdown and Best Combat Windows">

Villain Arc’s kit mixes melee strings, ranged bursts, and one very large finisher. The best results come from using control first, then spending the biggest damage tools when enemies are already locked in place.

<Callout type="info" title="Damage Pattern">
Treat the kit like a sequence: pressure, stun, burst, then finish. That rhythm matters more than raw button speed.
</Callout>

| Ability | Damage / Effect | Cooldown | Best Use |
|---|---|---|---|
| Baton combo | 1 punch, then 2 baton swings; **100 per hit** with red-energy stun | Not listed | Close-range pressure after a stun or in a crowded lane |
| Right-arm burst | Short burst of red energy; **100 damage** | **8 seconds** | Safe poke and quick follow-up damage |
| Plasma cannon | **420 total damage**; requires **Overkill Protocol** | **3 seconds** | Burst pressure when targets stay grouped |
| Soundwave boost | **75 damage** plus a major speed increase | **20 seconds** | Reposition, chase, or escape before committing |
| Death Ray upgrade | Stronger variant of **Death Ray** for **1 minute** | **2 minutes** | Save for extended fights and high-value targets |
| Orbital cannon | Gigantic laser; **8,000 damage** | **65 seconds** | Finisher for bosses, clustered lanes, or locked targets |
| Dark clones | Spawns **4** clones that explode on contact; **100 each** | **32 seconds** | Area denial and anti-move pressure |

| Priority | Use First | Why It Works |
|---|---|---|
| Open fight | Soundwave boost | Helps you reach the right angle before enemies split |
| Mid fight | Baton combo or right-arm burst | Maintains pressure while cooldowns cycle |
| High value window | Plasma cannon or orbital cannon | Converts control into real damage |
| Late fight | Dark clones | Punishes movement and narrows escape paths |

<Callout type="tip" title="Combat Tip">
The orbital cannon is strongest when enemies have already committed to a lane. If they are still spread out, spend smaller tools first.
</Callout>
