<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: How to Farm and Save Cores">

Follow this exact step-by-step procedure to ensure you farm efficiently and never lose a rare core drop due to a premature reset.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Casual Zombie Mode">
    Navigate to the main lobby. Select **Classic Mode**, choose **Casual**, and load into **Zombie Mode**. Avoid private servers for farming if you want to be completely safe from inventory saving glitches.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear Early Waves & Scan">
    Use your **Upgraded Camera Strider** or **Alarm Clock Man** to quickly defeat the incoming infected waves. While fighting, zoom out or go into first-person mode to scan the entire arena floor for a glowing blue aura.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Secure and Save the Core">
    If you spot a core, walk over it to pick it up. **Crucial**: You must complete the active wave you are currently on. If you reset or exit the lobby before the wave officially ends, the core will not save to your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Reset at Wave 7">
    If no cores have spawned by the end of **Wave 7**, press Esc and reset your character. Re-enter the lobby and queue back into Zombie Mode to restart the cycle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Boss Tactics: Zombie Titans

- **Zombie Upgraded Titan Speaker Man (Wave 10)**: Immune to normal attacks. You must target the specific **weak points** (like his hand) that appear every 60 seconds and deal 1,500 damage to break his defense.
- **Zombie Upgraded Titan TV Man (Wave 12)**: Features heavy armor. During phase 2, he takes only 25% damage, and in phase 3, a tiny 2.5% damage. Wait for him to scream, then shoot directly into his open mouth to trigger a **Break Effect** for 4x damage.

### Team Lineup for Wave 13 Clears

| Role | Character | Key Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Time Control** | **Large Clockman** | Use Time Stop to freeze dangerous infected swarms. |

| **DPS / Carry** | **Titan Clockman** | Coordinate so only one player deploys him; protect him from being targeted. |
